Welcome to Coco's Chocolatiers. We take pride in maintaining the only guerilla candy website on the internet. We don't have to deal with safety inspectors or labour rights so we pass the savings on to you in the form of delicious chocolate.

Orders can only be placed via snail mail. We don’t believe in e-commerce. To place an order, simply write down what you would like by hand. Cursive only. Mail it in with a self-addressed, stamped envelope and cash (CAD only) and you should receive your order in 4-6 weeks. 8-12 weeks for international orders. 6-12 months for interplanetary orders.

Shipping is flat-rate: $10 within Canada, $100 international, and $10,000 interplanetary.
