Orders can only be placed via snail mail. We don’t believe in e-commerce. To place an order, simply write down what you would like by hand. Cursive only. Mail it in with a self-addressed, stamped envelope and cash (CAD only) and you should receive your order in 4-6 weeks. 8-12 weeks for international orders. 6-12 months for interplanetary orders. Shipping is flat-rate: $10 within Canada, $100 international, and $10,000 interplanetary.
